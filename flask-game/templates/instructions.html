The Rules Are Simple:

After you enter a unique username, you will be presented with a pictorial puzzle that represents a common phrase.

You have three options: Guess the phrase correctly. Get it wrong. Or pass on it all together.


If You Answer Correctly:

Good Job! 50 points will be added to your score. You will move on to the next puzzle.


If You Get It Wrong:

Oops! You will lose 1 life. Remember you only have 5!

You will remain on this puzzle until you either get it right, pass on it or lose all your lives trying to guess it.


If You Pass:

You will move on to the next question.

You won't gain any points. But be careful, every three passes will result in a life being lost.


How The Game Ends:

If you lose all your lives or make it to the end of the quiz

Other Info

When entering your guess, using any punctuation marks will result in a wrong answer even if the phrase is correct.
